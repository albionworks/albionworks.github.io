PLASMON // OASIS




60" film for Italian food brand Plasmon. Plasmon is famous for producing nutritious and tasty baby food. The premise was to design a miniature world, an Oasis of Growth, filled with colourful characters who nurture and raise the food ingredients essential for a baby's nutrition and development. The story follows our hero, the Cricket, as he dances and whistles his way throughout the world, meeting a host of working companions, and of course lending a helping hand when he can. Featuring some fantastic character design from Psyop designer Velwyn Yossy.
